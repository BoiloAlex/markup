Chuck Thackstonlight

Managing Director, EIM, ARC

Chuck Thackston oversees the growth of business intelligence, analytical products and research at ARC. After leading the company's program to consolidate global ticketing data into ARC's COMPASS® database, Chuck is now searching for new ways to leverage ARC's vast data resources and experience.

Chuck brings more than 25 years of technology experience to ARC, with leadership roles in engineering, analytics, marketing and product development. His prior experience includes work in healthcare, airline and technology services industries at Travelport, Worldspan, Sabre, EDS and American Airlines. Chuck holds a BA in computer science from Tennessee and an Executive MBA from Texas Christian University.
