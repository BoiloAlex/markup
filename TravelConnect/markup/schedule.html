10:20am

The Mobile Traveler

General Session

Mobile has redefined the traveler's journey and changed the travel ecosystem with the advent of social commerce available at all stages of the cycle. PhoCusWright estimates that by 2015, mobile will account for one-quarter of all U.S. online travel sales, driving $40 billion in travel-related commerce via mobile. This panel discussion will examine how recent innovations in mobile will change the travel space in future and how this transformation will impact the industry globally.

Moderator: Arun Gupta Director Product Management, ARC Panelists: Glenn M. Gruber Sr. Mobility Strategist, Propelics, Inc. John Hibbard Chief Revenue Officer, FlightStats Evan Konwiser Co-Founder & COO, Lark Travel

11:10am

Using Data Effectively

General Session

Airlines have been using data to analyze operations for decades—as have many progressive agencies. But the use of this data is changing rapidly, and the scope of data available is growing along with it. This session will offer a comprehensive look at what data sources are available through both direct and indirect channel activity, including but not limited to: sales data, average and benchmarking fare (market) data, schedules and capacity data, and passenger accounting and historical data.

Chuck Thackston Managing Director, EIM, ARC
